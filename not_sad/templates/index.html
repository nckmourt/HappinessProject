Exploring The World Happiness Report

I feel that the American dream can be achieved best in the Nordic countries, where every child no matter their background or the background of their families can become anything.

Sanna Marin, the 34-year-old prime minister of Finland, to the Washington Post

According to Kendra Cherry's article on Very Well Mind, Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs - one of the best known theories of motivation - states that people generally need to fulfill the most basic needs, located at the bottom of the pyramid, first before progressing to more complex needs, located at the top of the pyramid.

Explore

The United Nations publishes the World Happiness Report annually, which ranks over 150 countries based on citizens' perceptions of happiness across various lifestyle measures; the measures are grouped into six factors, adding to each countries' overall score.

GDP per Capita • Social (and Family) Support  • Life Expectancy • Freedom • Generosity • Government Corruption
