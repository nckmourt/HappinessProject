Explore

Each year, the United Nations publishes the World Happiness Report ranks over 150 countries based on citizen perceptions of happiness across various lifestyle measures. These measures are analyzed and grouped into 6 factors adding to each countries overall score.

GDP per Capita • Social (and Family) Support  • Life Expectancy • Freedom • Generosity • Government Corruption
